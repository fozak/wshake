Optimize healthcare professionals schedules and manage HR & payroll

Keep your healthcare workforce agile and productive with ERPNext. Seamlessly manage doctor and nurse schedules, automate shift planning, and handle emergencies with real-time updates. Integrated alerts reduce conflicts and ensure smooth coordination. ERPNexts HR and Payroll modules further automate recruitment, attendance tracking, and payroll processing. Built-in tools for performance evaluations, leave approvals, and compliance tracking streamline HR operations, ensuring a happy and motivated workforce.

The system also automates employee health monitoring, immunization tracking, and certification renewals, keeping staff compliant with regulatory standards and ready for audits.

Track complete OPD and IPD workflows

Simplify outpatient (OPD) and inpatient (IPD) workflows from registration to discharge with ERPNext. Automate patient registration, treatment planning, bed assignments, and billing. Built-in templates allow seamless documentation of diagnoses, surgeries, and post-operative care.

Track patient encounters, clinical assessments, and follow-up appointments for continuity of care. ERPNexts Healthcare Service Units feature enables real-time tracking of bed occupancy, discharges, transfers, and cleaning schedules, optimizing bed utilization and turnaround times. Automated workflows eliminate manual errors, improve response times, and ensure better patient outcomes.

Integrated lab operations for pathology and radiology

ERPNext transforms lab management with automated workflows for sample tracking, test orders, and result delivery. Integrated imaging tools simplify radiology operations and streamline report generation, enabling faster collaboration between labs and physicians.
