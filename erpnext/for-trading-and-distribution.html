Why trading and distribution industry uses ERPNext?

Stay stocked, stay profitable: smart inventory management

Never lose a sale due to stockouts or tie up cash in excess inventory. ERPNexts real-time inventory tracking ensures you always have the right stock at the right place. Automate reorder levels, track batches and serial numbers, and optimize procurement to keep your business running smoothly.

Learn more

Hassle-free import and export: Simplify global trade

Managing international trade can be complex, but ERPNext makes it easy. Automate landed cost calculations, track duties and freight, and generate customs documentsall in one system. With built-in multi-currency support and compliance features, cross-border trading is seamless and error-free.

Learn more

Multi-warehouse, multi-location distributionall in one place

Effortlessly manage inventory across multiple warehouses and distribution centers. Automate stock transfers, track inbound and outbound shipments, and optimize fulfillment based on demand and location. Reduce delays and improve order accuracy with a system that thinks ahead.

Learn more

Sell everywhere, fulfill faster: omnichannel order management

Whether you sell B2B, B2C, or across multiple online and offline channels, ERPNext keeps everything in sync. Orders from eCommerce platforms, marketplaces, and direct sales channels are automatically processed with real-time stock updates. Bulk order fulfillment becomes effortless.

Learn more

Stronger supplier relationships with smarter procurement

Late deliveries and pricing issues can impact profits. ERPNext automates purchase requests, tracks vendor performance, and helps you negotiate better deals. Streamline sourcing and procurement to get the best suppliers at the best prices.

Learn more

Logistics & shipping made simple

Cut down on shipping delays and errors with ERPNexts integrated logistics management. Generate packing slips, delivery notes, and shipping labels in just a few clicks. Track shipments in real time, manage freight costs, and process returns smoothly with an automated RMA system.

Learn more

Smart pricing, discounts and customer credit management

Maximize profitability with dynamic pricing and discount strategies. Whether its tiered pricing, bulk discounts, or seasonal offers, ERPNext gives you full control. Manage customer credit limits, automate invoicing, and keep cash flow steady with real-time payment tracking.

Learn more

Payments without the paperwork: Automated reconciliation

Eliminate manual tracking of payments and invoices. ERPNext reconciles sales, payments, and bank transfers automatically, ensuring your accounts are always accurate. For global trade, forex adjustments and multi-currency handling are built-in.

Learn more

Give customers & suppliers ccontrol with a self-service portal

Empower your customers to track orders, download invoices, and manage returns without calling support. Suppliers can update inventory, receive purchase orders, and track paymentsreducing back-and-forth emails and increasing efficiency.
