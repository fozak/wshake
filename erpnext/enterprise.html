ERPNext is popular among large enterprises—and why shouldn't it be? Over the past 17 years, it has evolved into a Swiss army knife that perfectly suits the complex needs of large enterprises across all industries. That is why we have some of the largest companies in the world choose ERPNext over SAP, Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and more.

Why enterprises choose ERPNext?

Lower total cost of ownership

Customers who choose ERPNext lower the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) of ERP from 2-6% of their revenue to less than 0.5%. The TCO includes licensing, customizations, implementation, hosting, training, support and upgrade costs.

No vendor lock-in

With SAP nudging its customers to S/4 Hana, customers want to end their long standing vendor lock-in. But if you move from one vendor you're at a risk of getting locked in with another one. ERPNext is 100% open-source with a large network of global partners, allowing you to end vendor lock-in with peace of mind.

Customisable and scalable

ERPNext can be customized to fit your needs. With ERPNext, making small tweaks like custom fields and reports takes seconds (not weeks), and complex changes can be achieved with simple client/server scripts. This is made possible with the underlying web framework. To top that—with Frappe Cloud’s custom app deployment architecture, you can build modular customizations and scale them easily.

Flexible to host

ERPNext can be hosted anywhere, on-premise/on-cloud hosting to align with your enterprise's infrastructure strategy. This flexibility ensures compliance with local regulations and enterprise IT policies. We recommend hosting on Frappe Cloud's secure and reliable hosting to unlock the ease of managed hosting, but you can choose to host anywhere you like.

User-friendly

With an intuitive interface, ERPNext enhances user adoption and reduces training time. It empowers teams to work efficiently, improving productivity across the organization.

Frappe Cloud enterprise hosting benefits

Frappe Cloud is a secure, scalable, and reliable hosting platform specially built for managed hosting of Frappe apps. It simplifies upgrades, backups, custom app deployments, and more. Frappe Cloud comes with a first-party warranty for Frappe applications like ERPNext, FrappeHR, and Frappe Framework.

Enterprise-grade security

Frappe Cloud is powered by multiple layers of security to ensure your data is protected. Both ERPNext and Frappe Cloud are open-source, which means more eyes and hands working on it than black box proprietary software.
