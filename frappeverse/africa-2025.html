Meet the ecosystem

Frappeverse is a great place to meet the local Frappe ecosystem of independent service providers, developers and freelancers.

If you need help with your projects, you are mostly likely to find the right person to help you out. If you are a Frappe service provider, the conference is a great place to meet like-minded folks and also explore collaboration opportunities, not to mention meeting customers.

Product Keynote

Every year, the Frappe team along with the community showcases things they have been working on for the past year and plan to release in the near future.

The Frappe ecosystem has together created over 200 apps and some of these will be showcased at Frappeverse Africa.

Community stories

It is always exciting to hear how an ever expanding global community of users is using Frappe products to solve their and their customers problems.

Expect to hear a wide range of stories from technology oriented entrepreneurs to freelancers to folks who work with non profits and governments in Africa.
