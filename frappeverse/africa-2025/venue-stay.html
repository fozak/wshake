Venue and stay

In order to make your journey and experience during the conference better, here are some details that will help you with the stay and travel.

Reaching the venue

By Air

If you are arriving by air, the nearest airport is Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA). From the airport, you can take an airport taxi or Uber to reach the venue. Please consider 50-60 minutes for travel time to venue from the airport, considering traffic conditions in Nairobi.

By Bus

If you wish to take public transport, first head to Nairobi Central Business District (CBD). Locate Koja Stage within the CBD, where buses to Lower Kabete are available. Look for a bus going to University of Nairobi, Lower Kabete. Confirm with the conductor that it will pass by the Kenya School of Government.Inform the conductor to drop you at the Kenya School of Government.

By Private Transport

You can book a cab using the Uber app for a convenient trip to the venue. And if you will be travelling by your own vehicle, use Google Maps for navigation to reach the Kenya School of Government, Lower Kabete. Parking is available on-site.
