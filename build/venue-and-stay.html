Venue and stay

In order to make your journey and experience during the conference better, here are some details that will help you with the stay and travel.

Reaching the venue

By Air

If you are arriving by air, the nearest airport is Kempegowda International Airport (BLR). From the airport, you can book a taxi or an Uber to reach Koramangala Club. Travel time to the venue is approximately 1.5–2 hours, depending on traffic conditions in Bangalore.

By Train

The nearest railway stations are:

  • Bangalore City Railway Station (Majestic) – 10 km from the venue.

  • Yesvantpur Junction – 16 km from the venue.

From either station, you can book a cab through Uber or Ola to reach Koramangala Club. Alternatively, auto-rickshaws are also available at the stations for shorter rides. Travel time will vary based on traffic, but it generally takes 30–45 minutes.

By Private Transport

For a hassle-free journey, book a cab using Uber or Ola. If you're traveling in your own vehicle, use Google Maps to navigate to Koramangala Club.
