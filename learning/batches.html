Batches

Some learners prefer to study at their own pace, while others learn better in a group with a set schedule. Thats where batches come in. It allows you to organize learners into groups taking the same courses at the same time. This makes it easier to manage their progress, tests, live sessions, and communication, all in one place.

Create and manage batches

Whether you're running a short workshop or a semester-long course, Batches give you the control you need. Set start and end dates, decide how many learners can join, describe what the batch is about, and categorize them for easy finding. If you charge for your courses, you can even set the price, and learners can enroll after paying.

Enroll students and assign courses

Managing course enrollments shouldn't give you a headache. With batches in Frappe Learning, you control every aspect of your program - from setting start dates to deciding class sizes that work for your teaching approach. Write clear descriptions so students know exactly what they're joining, and group similar courses together to help them find what they need. If you offer premium courses, you can set prices and let students enroll automatically after payment, saving you from manual payment tracking.

Schedule and manage assessments

Tracking assignments and quizzes often means jumping between multiple tools and spreadsheets. Our assessment system brings everything into one place. You'll see all submissions, can review work, and give feedback right where students can find it. Students know exactly what they need to do and when it's due, while you maintain a clear view of how everyone's progressing.

Conduct live classes

Online teaching works best when you can focus on your students, not the technology. Schedule Zoom sessions right from your course page, and your students will get calendar invites automatically. They join class directly from their dashboard - no hunting for links or passwords. It's teaching that feels natural, just like being in a real classroom.

Send announcements via emails

Important updates should reach everyone who needs them. Send messages to your whole class or specific groups directly from your course page. Instead of managing email lists manually, you can reach your entire batch with just a few clicks. Your students get the information they need, right when they need it.

Facilitate doubts resolution

Every class needs a space where students can help each other learn. Each batch gets its own discussion area where questions find answers and ideas spark conversations. Students can ask for help anytime, share what they've learned, and learn from their classmates. You can guide these discussions easily, keeping everything relevant and organized. It's like having study groups that never end, making learning more engaging for everyone.
