Frappe Support Portal

To use the support portal, you must first sign up for an account. To do so, go to the signup page and login with your Frappe Cloud account by clicking on "Login with Frappe Cloud" button.


Raise a Ticket

Once you have successfully registered on the support portal, you can raise a ticket.

  1. Go to the My Tickets page and click on the New Ticket button.



  2. Select the App that you are facing an issue with. You will be able to raise tickets only for the apps you have purchased support for.

  3. Select the Site. If you cannot see your site, you need to register your site (see below).

  4. Enter the issue in detail. We also encourage you to attach screenshots of the issue so that we could understand it better.

  5. Click on Create.


Site Registration

In order to raise tickets for your site, you will first have to register it for us to validate if you are an active user on the site.

To do so:

  1. Go to support.frappe.io/register-site.

  2. Enter your site name, email id, and password. Your email id and password will be the same as the one you have set for the site. You should be an Active, System User and your site should be under a valid Frappe Subscription.



  3. Once you complete the registration process, you can select the site and raise a ticket against it.

If you face any issues with the new portal, you can contact us at support@erpnext.com.
