Frappeverse

Welcome

Welcome to Frappeverse, the vibrant community where ideas, creativity, and technology come together. This is more than just a conference—it's a celebration of the innovative spirit driving Frappe and its ecosystem forward.

Whether you're a developer, business owner, or an open-source enthusiast, Frappeverse is where you will get inspired to explore open-source solutions, and push the boundaries of what we can create together. Let's dive into a world of endless possibilities!

Africa 2025

After years of hosting successful conferences in India, Frappe expanded its reach to bring the local community together.

Frappeverse Africa 2025 offered a chance to learn, share knowledge, and build lasting connections in the heart of Africa.
