Story of Frappe School

Back in the day, ERPNext was Frappe's flagship product. An ERP by nature is a complex product, it solves difficult business problems. Even though ERPs have existed for more than three decades now, users still find it difficult to operate them. Despite having extensive documentation around products, users contact customer support for issues they might easily be able to handle.

It was the first quarter of 2021 at Frappe, busy, just like any other quarter. After solving all customer queries firsthand, there were discussions at Frappe on the lack of good resources in the ERP domain. The team decided that there is a need to have a learning platform that is focused on ERP implementations for various kinds of ERP users. Frappe has been working on, maintaining, and implementing ERPNext since 2008. We decided to have our own learning platform, leading to the creation of Frappe School.

Since then, Frappe has become much more than ERPNext. We now have a universe of products we call the Frappeverse. With this, Frappe School has also expanded to a plethora of courses and trainings. In the future, we want Frappe School to be the go-to learning platform for all engineers and consultants.
