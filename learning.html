Frappe Learning is a 100% open-source platform that helps you create structured courses with videos, quizzes, and assignments—perfect for educators, businesses, and content creators. Run live batches, track progress, and build a learning experience that works for you.

Story of Frappe Learning

"While the pandemic was a difficult time for us all, it brought with it an opportunity to democratize learning. With massive improvements in internet access, education became more accessible than ever. To do our part, FOSS United and Frappe decided to launch Mon School, an online platform where anyone could learn to code for free. But we couldn’t find an LMS that fit our needs, so we decided to build it from scratch.

Shortly after Mon School was released, our team at Frappe realized that we needed a learning platform of our own. Our main offering at the time was ERPNext, and ERP systems are inherently complex, so we decided to create Frappe School to help our community understand it better. But this time, instead of starting from square one, I separated out the Mon School-specific customizations and used the underlying app to build Frappe School.

I realized then that I was onto something. The underlying app was easy enough to use and customize that anyone could use it to launch their own learning platform. So I decided to turn it into its own product, and that’s how Frappe Learning was born."

Product Engineer
